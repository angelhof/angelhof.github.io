Programmable Software Systems Lab at UCLA

The goal of our research is to enable the development of high-performance applications with robust correctness guarantees We achieve this by building practical programmable software systems that target realistic workloads in widely-used environments. Our systems are built on solid foundations using techniques drawn from the programming languages, compilers, and formal methods literature.
